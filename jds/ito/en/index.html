What is Johren?

A local discovery system designed for Japan.

Johren Adventure lets you explore
stations, shops, scenic viewpoints, and small cultural spots
while collecting stamps along the way.

No app. No account.
Your everyday commute or walk becomes a small journey.
QR stamp at each station
Short stories & local trivia

Chikuhi Line Spots

A few highlights along the route.

Starting Spot

Nokonoshima View

A calm seaside view. Scanning the QR gives you the “First Step” stamp.

Local Spot

Kyudai Tsutaya

A warm evening landmark where students and locals cross paths.

Seaside

Futamigaura (Meoto-Iwa)

Itoshima’s iconic coastline. Its scenery changes with the season.

Local View

Itoshima Morning

A quiet sunrise above fields and hills — the gentle beginning of the day.

Station Spot

Chikuhi Line Piano

A public piano anyone can play. A small place to feel the slow rhythm of the line.

Quiet Paths

Niji-no-Matsubara

One of Japan’s three great pine forests — a long sandy road framed by twisting pine trees.

History

Karatsu Castle

A scenic viewpoint overlooking the town and sea. The QR opens a short historical story.

Festival

Karatsu Kunchi

Each November, giant floats race through the streets. The QR opens a mini-guide.

How It Works

Very simple — no app required.

1
Find a Johren QR Marker

You’ll see Johren QR plates at stations from Meinohama to Karatsu.

2
Scan with Your Phone

Camera or browser is fine. No login or app needed.

3
Collect Your Stamp

A stamp and a small story opens, matching the place you scanned.

Features of Johren

Designed for Japan’s cultural logic.

A System Familiar to Japan

Stamp rallies, goshuin, point cards — Johren builds on things people already enjoy.

Experience, Not Money

Phase 1 has no discounts or exchange. The focus is simply walking, noticing, and enjoying.

Shops Can Join Easily

No setup fee. A single QR plate increases visits with almost no work required.

Start your journey at the next station.

Whether at Meinohama, Karatsu, or anywhere in between — if you see a Johren QR marker, that’s your starting point.
