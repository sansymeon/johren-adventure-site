For Places

Johren is a quiet, community-based walking project.
Nothing is required from people or places.

Most locations are simply listed as part of a local walking context. No setup, no participation, and no ongoing involvement is expected.

Sharing Johren

Sharing Johren is closer to recommending a good book than promoting a service.

There is no personal benefit, no responsibility, and no expectation that anyone explains or endorses it.

About QR codes

Some places may choose, on their own initiative, to share a little more information using a QR code.

This is optional and never required. Many places never use QR codes at all.

What Johren avoids

  • No advertising or solicitation
  • No tracking of individuals
  • No incentives or rewards
  • No obligation to explain or participate

For caf√©s, shops, and public spaces

Being included in Johren does not require any action.

Listings exist as quiet records only, and can be removed at any time upon request.

Johren exists to respect places, not to activate them.

If you have questions or would prefer not to be included, this can always be addressed.
