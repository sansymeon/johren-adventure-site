Johren Adventure

Johren Adventure is a digital platform that connects Japan’s
everyday life, travel, and local culture through simple QR moments.

From sightseeing and small local shops to child safety check-ins,
Johren brings together safety, discovery, and community activation
in one connected system.

Johren Discovery Spots

A gentle exploration system where scanning QR markers
earns stamps and unlocks small stories.

Johren Business Spot
