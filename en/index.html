Johren Discovery Spots

A gentle way to explore Japan

Johren Discovery Spots (JDS) is a simple QR-based exploration project that lets you rediscover towns, stations, and local landmarks through small, joyful “Journey Mark” stamps.

Scan a code, collect a stamp, and enjoy Japan through quiet moments of curiosity and delight.
