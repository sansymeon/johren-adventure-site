Discovery Maps

Part of Johren Adventure
Johren is a quiet discovery system designed for everyday places.

It does not require an app, registration, or explanation.
People simply notice a place, pause briefly, and continue walking.

What begins as a small moment of discovery can also support
local presence and community life — without changing
how places normally operate.
Some locations include simple markers, but discovery always comes first.
