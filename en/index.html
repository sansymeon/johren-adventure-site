Johren Adventure

Johren is a quiet, QR-based system designed for everyday places in Japan.

It does not require an app, registration, or explanation.
People simply scan, notice, and continue walking.

What begins as a small discovery can also support
local shops, community presence, and child safety —
without changing how places normally operate.

Johren Discovery

A gentle walking-and-discovery map.
QR markers unlock small stories tied to stations,
streets, and everyday landmarks.
