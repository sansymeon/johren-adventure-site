About Johren Opt-In

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Johren Opt-In?

Johren Opt-In allows cafés, shops, studios, stations, and other local places to quietly appear on the Johren Discovery map. Visitors discover places naturally while walking, using simple QR codes — not advertisements, promotions, or recommendations.

Is Johren a marketing or advertising service?

No. Johren does not run advertisements, rankings, reviews, or promotional campaigns. It is not designed to drive traffic or influence behavior. Johren exists to help places be noticed as part of a local journey.

What does participation involve?

Participation is intentionally minimal. A small Johren QR plate is placed at the location with permission. When visitors scan it, the place appears as part of their exploration. There are no apps to install and no ongoing actions required.

Is registration or personal information required?

No registration is required at this stage. Johren does not request personal details, customer data, or operational information. Participation begins with simple permission to place a QR code.

Does Johren involve fees or contracts?

There are no contracts at this stage. Any future changes will be communicated clearly and only with explicit agreement. Johren is being developed carefully, place by place, with respect for local context.
