For Business Owners

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Johren Business Spots?

Johren Business Spots (JBS) allows local cafés, shops, studios, and small businesses to appear on the Johren Discovery map. Visitors discover places naturally while walking, using quiet QR codes — not ads or promotions.

Is Johren a marketing or advertising service?

No. Johren does not run advertisements, rankings, reviews, or promotional campaigns. It is not designed to drive traffic through incentives. Johren exists to help everyday places be noticed as part of a local journey.

What does participation involve?

Participation is intentionally simple. A small Johren QR plate is placed at your location. When visitors scan it, your place appears as part of their exploration. There are no apps to install and no ongoing tasks required.

Do I need to sign a contract or provide business information?

No contracts are required at this stage. Johren does not request customer data, sales figures, or personal information. Business participation is introduced gradually and locally.

Does Johren cost money?

Details about costs, if any, will be shared clearly when business participation is formally opened. Johren is being developed carefully, place by place, with respect for local communities and businesses.
