What is Johren?

A local discovery system designed for Japan.

Johren Adventure is a quiet exploration system that connects stations, shops, scenic views, and small cultural places along everyday routes.

No app required. No registration.
Your usual commute or walk becomes a small journey.
QR stamps at each station
Short stories & local notes

How It Works

Very simple. No app required.

1. Find a QR marker

QR plates are placed at stations between Meinohama and Karatsu.

2. Scan with your phone

Your camera or browser is enough. No login needed.

3. Collect a journey mark

A short story and a place-specific stamp appear.

Start with the Itoshima map.

This area is a demonstration. New pins will be added gradually.
