What is Johren?

A local discovery system designed for Japan.

Johren Adventure is a quiet exploration system that connects stations, shops, scenic views, and small cultural places along your everyday routes.

No app required. No registration.
Your usual commute or walk becomes a small journey.
QR stamps at each station

How It Works

Very simple. No app required.

1. Find a QR marker

QR plates are placed at stations between Meinohama and Karatsu.

2. Scan with your phone

Your camera or browser is enough. No login needed.

3. Collect a journey mark

A short story and a place-specific stamp appear.

Why Johren?

Designed to fit Japanese cultural logic.

Familiar cultural patterns

Stamp rallies, goshuin, point cards — Johren builds on things people already enjoy.

Experience over rewards

Phase one focuses on walking, noticing, and remembering — not discounts or exchanges.

Easy for local places

No setup costs. A single QR plate allows visitors to discover places naturally.
