What is Johren?

A local discovery system designed for Japan.

Johren Adventure lets you move through
stations, shops, scenic views, and small cultural places,
collecting stamps as you go.

No app. No registration.
Your normal commute or walk becomes a small “journey.”

How it works

Very simple. No app needed.

1. Find a QR marker

Near each station in West Fukuoka, you’ll find a Johren QR plate.

2. Scan with your phone

Your camera or browser is enough. No login. No app installation.

3. Collect a stamp

You’ll unlock a stamp and a small story connected to that place.

Why Johren works

Designed to match Japan’s cultural logic.

Familiar in Japan

Stamp rallies, goshuin, point cards—Johren builds on cultures people already enjoy.

Experience first

In Phase 1, there are no discounts or exchanges. The focus is walking, noticing, and enjoying.

Easy for shops

Zero setup cost. One small QR plate can quietly bring more visitors—without extra work.
