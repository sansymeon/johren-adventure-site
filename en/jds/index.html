Johren Discovery Spots

Johren Discovery Spots (JDS) is a gentle exploration project using QR codes placed around towns, stations, and local landmarks.

Scan a code, collect a “Journey Mark” stamp, and rediscover Japan through small moments of curiosity and delight.
