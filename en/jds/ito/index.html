What is Johren Adventure?

A simple QR-based discovery system.

Johren Adventure is a Japan-style “local discovery stamp rally.” Scan a QR placed at stations, shops, and viewpoints to unlock stories, photos, and a small stamp for your journey. No app, no signup — just explore.

2
Scan

No app, no login — just use your camera.

3
Unlock
