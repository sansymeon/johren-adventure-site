What is Johren?

A local discovery system created for Japan.



What Johren values most is
“places that aren’t tourist spots, but shouldn’t be forgotten.”
Johren Adventure is a quiet local discovery system that connects
stations, shops, scenic views, and small cultural places
as you move through everyday routes.

No app required. No registration.
Your usual commute or walk becomes a small journey.
QR stamps at each station
Small stories & local notes
No app · no login

Everyday scenes beside the subway

Nature in the city

Ohori Park

A calm stretch of water in the heart of Chuo Ward, surrounded by buildings. Its mood changes between morning and evening, and for locals, it is simply part of everyday life.

A park on the hill

Nishi Park

A slightly elevated park just beside Ohori. Less a sightseeing spot, more a place locals use for walking, resting, and passing time.

The local team

PayPay Dome and the Sea

Home to Fukuoka’s local team, a place many people have visited again and again.
Even on days without a game, the sea breeze continues to blow.

How It Works

Very simple. No app required.

1. Find a QR marker
2. Scan with your phone

Your camera or browser is enough. No login needed.

3. Collect a journey mark

A short story and a place-specific stamp appear.

Start with the Fukuoka Center map.

This area is a demonstration. New pins will be added gradually.
