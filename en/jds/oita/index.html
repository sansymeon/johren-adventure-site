How Johren Works

Simple. No app required.

1. Find the QR Marker

Each participating station has a Johren Spot QR plate.

2. Scan with Your Phone

Use your camera or browser — no login needed.

3. Unlock a Stamp

A small story appears, along with your new stamp.

Why Johren?

Designed for Japan’s cultural logic.

Created for Local Exploration

Small places, light walking, and easy discoveries — a very Yufuin rhythm.

Experience, Not Discounts

The joy is in the scenery, history, and atmosphere — not transactions.

No Burden for Shops

No setup cost. No registration. A single QR plate is all they need.
