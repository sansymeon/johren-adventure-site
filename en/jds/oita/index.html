How It Works

What is Johren?

A discovery system made for Japan.

Johren Adventure is a local discovery system where you
visit stations, shops, viewpoints, and small cultural spots
and collect stamps as you go.

No app. No registration.
Just your everyday commute or walk becoming a small personal journey.
QR stamps at each station
Unlock mini stories & local insights
No app and no signup needed

Spots on the Line

Highlights along the Chikuhi Line.

START SPOT

Nokonoshima View

Bright spring colors overlooking the sea. Scanning the QR reveals your “first step“ stamp.

NEIGHBORHOOD

Kyudai Bookstore

A warm night-time landmark where students and locals cross paths.

COASTLINE

Futamigaura (Meoto Iwa)

One of Itoshima’s iconic seaside destinations, changing color with every season.

LOCAL VIEW

A quiet sunrise behind fields and mountains — the soft start of a journey.

STATION SPOT

A street piano anyone can play — a small reminder of the slow pace of the Chikuhi Line.

QUIET WALK

HISTORY

A sweeping view of the sea and town. The QR unlocks a short local history story.

FESTIVAL

Karatsu Kunchi Floats

Gigantic festival floats racing through the streets every November. The QR reveals a mini-guide.

How Johren Works

Simple. No app required.

1. Find the QR Marker

Each station from Meinohama to Karatsu has a Johren Spot QR plate.

2. Scan with Your Phone

Use your camera or browser. No login or app required.

3. Unlock a Stamp

You gain a new stamp and a small story about the place.

Why Johren?

Designed for Japan’s cultural logic.

Built on Familiar Japanese Systems

Stamp rallies, goshuin, and point cards — Johren uses what people already love.

Experience, Not Money

No discounts or redemptions in Phase 1. The joy is in walking, seeing, and discovering.

Easy for Shops to Join

No setup cost. A single QR plate brings visitors without adding workload.

Begin your adventure at the next station.

Start from anywhere — Meinohama, Karatsu, or in between. If you see a Johren Spot QR, your journey begins there.
