For those introduced by a player

Johren is a local walking and discovery system that helps people notice places and shops they may not have known about in their everyday surroundings.

It can feel a little like a stamp rally, encouraging small discoveries throughout the neighbourhood.

There is no cost involved. No registration or contracts are required. It simply offers a gentle reason to look at familiar streets from a slightly different angle.

  • • This is not an app
  • • No registration is required
  • • There are no contracts
  • • There is no cost
  • • No personal information is collected
