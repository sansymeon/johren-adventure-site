Discovery Spots

"Discovery Spots" is a gentle exploration project that invites you to notice the places you might normally walk past.

Simple QR codes are quietly placed near cultural landmarks, streets, and local merchants. Scan one to collect a “Discovery Mark” — a small personal trace of having been there — and rediscover your town through moments of curiosity, stillness, and delight.

Kota Kinabalu's Gaya Street Area

A coastal town shaped by history, the blending of various cultures, and everyday life. Discover the city centre of Kota Kinabalu, in historic "Old Town Jestleton," where the quiet streets, and small corners still remain.
