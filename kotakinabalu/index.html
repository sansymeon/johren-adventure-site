Discovery Spots

Discovery Spots is a quiet, QR-based system designed for everyday places in Japan.

It does not require an app, registration, or explanation.
People simply scan, notice, and continue walking.

What begins as a small discovery can also support
local shops and community presence—
without changing how places normally operate.
